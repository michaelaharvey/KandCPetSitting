Welcome to Killy and Carter's Pet Sitting

Hello, my name is Nicki and I am a teacher and an animal lover. In my free time, I offer pet sitting services in the Lake Forest and Mission Viejo areas.

Many dogs can become anxious when left alone, especially at night. I offer reassurance that your pet will not be alone, and will have a great time in my care. Whether your are planning a night out, or leaving for the weekend, my service will provide the peace of mind that your beloved pet is well cared for.

Appointments

I can pet-sit with your pet at your home, or you can leave your pet with me at my home. Whichever works best for you. The price for my service is $15 per hour for pet sitting from my home, and $20 per hour to pet-sit from your home.

About

I am the proud parent of two labradors, a terrier, and two cats, and I love my pets very much!
